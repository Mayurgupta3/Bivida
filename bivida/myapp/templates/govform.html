Instructions to Tenders

Completion of Tender
All information should clearly state the tender’s name, the tender reference number and the section and details to which 
 the response relates.

Form of Tender - Complete and Return.

Tenderer’s Submission - Complete by inserting all of the detail requested. Your answers should relate only to the Tender 
 and method of recompense set out in the Specification below and in Part- Contract Documents.
     Basic Details - Other information required by the Institution
     Work Item Details - Alternative Proposals (where permitted)
     Critical Dates  - Qualification of the Contract
You are not required to submit any other details.

Basic Details
Section I of this Appendix forms part of the offer made in the Form of Tender.
Section II is for additional information not supplied elsewhere:
      (i) Provide information of your latest 2-years published accounts or for the period that is available if trading 
 for less than 2 years.
      (ii) If you wish, supply any other information you feel might legitimately support your tender.
Note: You should carefully read the following terms & condition.

Work Item Details
Include under this Appendix any alternative proposals you wish to submit for consideration, Qualification of the Contract.

Critical Dates -
Include here details of any areas where you will not be able to comply with the contract as set out in this Invitation 
 to Tender. Qualifications should only 
 be made if you cannot comply with the requirements of the contract on time.
